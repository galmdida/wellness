What are the benefits of fasting?

I used to have a dog pet, whom I call Kebue. I used to cook some porriage for Kebue in the evening. Cool it, and give. This has been my evening routine for like three years. One day, I prepared the normal meal and poured to Kebue's service pot. He did not move from where he was lying. Staring at me god knows what he was thinking. I was fearful for his life. Some dogs around were contracting rabbies and doubted ones were getting poisoned. I ran to mum and explained. Barerly holding my lips not to cry and beg to spare his life. She never liked him, you know. Mum smiled and said, "your Kebue will be fine. He has not been going around like other dogs." That was a relief for me and my poor Kebue. Then she added, "Dogs sometimes don't feel like eating".

Fortunately, food is available. We can eat and drink whatever we want at anytime. Good news. The bad news is that, we do not listen to our body. Have you ever felt like you don't wanna eat? I bet you felt, I can as well bet with this conversation that you eat/ drink while you don't feel like. You have snacks in the fridge, freinds are calling for dinners, and drinks are perfectly displayed on the supermarket counters. It is difficult not to eat. Who is better? My Kebue or the humans with brains.

I should stop here for today. Give you some time to think more on this. I will discuss the specific benefits that I have discovered over the years in our next episode. Meanwhile, eat only when you feel like. Learn to listen to your body. Ramadhan Mubark to you!
