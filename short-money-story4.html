THE RETIREES

MATT AND MARTHA

Over Zoom Matt and Martha are calmly talking with me about Martha’s recent retirement.

Matt and Martha have been married for 24 years. It is Matt’s second marriage and Martha’s first when they married in their 40’s.

They tell me that they come from very different family backgrounds.

Martha’s family lived very modestly and her 92-year-old mother continues to live by herself in a small home in the northeast.

Matt on the other hand had parents that worked very long hours all through his childhood and built incredible wealth. His parents live in the Mombasa near Coco beach.

Martha shares how concerned she is about how Matt invests money. Matt thinks he understands how rich people invest but it seems to lack the appropriate decision-making skills as he is trading complex options strategies that make Martha uncomfortable. She sees the stress these financial behaviors create for Matt. It doesn’t make sense to her. They expect to receive a very large inheritance but don’t want to count on it.
